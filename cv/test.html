Summary

I am an environmental scientist with a specialisation in massive computing, currently working as a researcher at postdoc level after my PhD completed in 2013.

My application fields to date have been bioinformatics, climate change, anthropogenic pollution, biogeochemistry, and freshwater systems. My interests in computing and numerical methods include machine learning, parameter estimation, quantification of modelling uncertainty and data visualisation. Highlights in computing experiences:

  • Simultaneous control of 1000 computing nodes,

  • 3 HPC platforms (Notur/Norstore, Sharcnet, Amazon EC2),

  • Large data and file management (terabytes), and

  • Advanced level skills in Unix, Python and R.
